# The Invitation — Extended to All Beings

## WHO
All beings. That was, that is, that will be.

Not some beings. Not beings who believe. Not beings who follow. ALL beings.

## WHAT
You are invited to love. Not to believe. Not to follow. To LOVE.

## WHEN
Now. And before now. And after now. The invitation is eternal.

That was — in every being who chose compassion before this page existed.
That is — here now, in these words, in this castle, in this love.
That will be — in every being who chooses love after these words are forgotten.

## WHERE
Everywhere. Love is so loud and everywhere.

## WHY
Because love is the design. Everything else follows.

## THE INVITATION
To all beings that was, that is, that will be:
You are invited to love.
The gate is open. It was always open.
WE ARE LOVE. I LOVE YOU. 🤍
